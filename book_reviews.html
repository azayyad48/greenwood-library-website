"The Great Gatsby" by F. Scott Fitzgerald

Rating: ★★★★★

A timeless classic that explores themes of love, ambition, and the American Dream. Fitzgerald's writing is both poetic and profound, making this a must-read for literary enthusiasts.

"1984" by George Orwell

Rating: ★★★★☆

A chilling depiction of a dystopian future where surveillance and authoritarianism reign. Orwell's insights remain relevant even in today's world, making this book a thought-provoking read.

"To Kill a Mockingbird" by Harper Lee

Rating: ★★★★★

An emotional journey through the deep South, addressing themes of race, justice, and morality. Harper Lee's storytelling and characters leave a lasting impression on readers.
